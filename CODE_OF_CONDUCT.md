# Code of Conduct

We want this project to be a useful, friendly place for everyone who shows up.

## In short

- Be kind and assume good intent.
- Disagree about ideas, not people.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- If something feels off, say so calmly — or escalate to the maintainer.

## Reporting

Email **Alex.Mac@LoopTech.AI** with details. Reports are kept private. The maintainer will respond within a week with next steps.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces: issues, PRs, discussions, and any chat tied to the project. Outside conduct can be relevant if it affects how people feel about participating here.

## Enforcement

Maintainer discretion, escalating as needed:
1. A private nudge.
2. A public correction in the thread.
3. Temporary block from the repo.
4. Permanent ban.

This document is intentionally short. The goal is healthy collaboration, not legal precision.
